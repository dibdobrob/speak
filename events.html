Where and when are SPEAK.events taking place?

The spring 'seedling' series will be taking place from early March in Manchester. As the name might suggest, the 'seedling' series will be focussed on giving the previously unheard a stage upon which they can speak, learn and grow. If you are interested in being a seedling, listening to what our new speakers have to say or providing support and guidance to our seedlings then please get intouch.
